How to contribute

The documentation team works on Freepto documentation and on small guides for the tools installed on Freepto that are useful for the user security.

This team also works on transalting the documentation.

The main English document is the wiki, hosted on https://we.riseup.net/.

If you wanto to contribute to the project you can subscribe to general mailing-list.
